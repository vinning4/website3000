// About Me

I'm Vin. With a background in systems biology and product design, I approach web development with a function-first mindset — creating sites designed to turn visitors into customers.

Based in Melbourne AU, I run an independent practice that helps service businesses win more customers. I work with you directly to build high-performance, hand-coded websites — minus the typical agency markup and jargon.

// About Websites

The true measure of a business website is performance, not complexity. Yet most sites are built once and forgotten. Slow on mobile. Invisible to Google. Designed to show existence rather than serve a visitor. Quietly, they degrade into a liability dressed as an asset.

The prevailing market options cause this. On one end are bloated, template-driven WordPress builds that fail at the technical basics. On the other are high-dollar agencies encumbered by process, overhead, and jargon. Both miss the point.

An effective website is a precise commercial instrument that helps the right audience find you, understand your offer, and act. For most businesses, this requires just 3 to 5 fast, well-structured pages.

A third path is DIY. While publishing a page is now easy, true effectiveness requires technical discipline—ensuring a site attracts qualified visitors, loads instantly, and ranks on Google. Done right, it becomes a durable asset.

// The Work

Website3000 is different. Every site is hand-coded — no templates, no plugins, no unnecessary complexity. I focus on what matters: fast-loading websites designed to capture enquiries and render accurately on any device. I write every line of code in semantic HTML, CSS and JavaScript — an increasingly uncommon approach that allows direct control over the look and functionality. The result is a simpler, more dependable system: no bloated layers, no databases to hack, no plugins that crash.

The subscription model exists because a website is not finished at launch. It is a living operation that should evolve with a business. Edits, SEO, performance monitoring, and ongoing management are included — not as extras, but as the point.

// The Practice

Clients get a site that loads in one second, scores 95–100 on Google PageSpeed, and is designed to turn visitors into enquiries. You also work directly with me as I manage your site — no call queues, no support tickets, no handoffs.

This is independent practice at its most deliberate — each client served well, and completely. Not scaled for volume. Calibrated for quality.
