  • About Your Site: First Impressions Matter

    Good website design turns visitors into buyers. Most businesses just need 3 to 5 clean, fast-loading pages but the two options disappoint: Cut-rate 'WordPress experts' who commoditize bloated templates, or pricey agencies flaunting jargon.

    Both miss the point — your website isn't a vanity project, it's digital real estate working 24/7 to build trust.

    Yes, anyone can 'have a website' using DIY builders — this is like printing a sign to stick above your shop. It proves you exist, but does it do anything to find customers and guide them to buy? Does it appear before competitors' sites? How fast does it load? And on mobiles? Have you told Google how to find and rank your site?

    These things matter if you want your website to actually bring in customers. A strategically built, SEO-driven site is more like having a trained sales team : it helps the right people find you, answers their questions, and guides them to contact you.
