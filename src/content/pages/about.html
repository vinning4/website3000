// About Me

I'm Vin. With a background in systems biology and product design, I approach web development with a function-first mindset — creating sites designed to turn visitors into customers.

Based in Melbourne AU, I run an independent practice to help service businesses win more customers. I work with you directly to build high-performance, hand-coded websites — minus the typical agency markup, meetings and jargon.

// About Websites

I see too many business websites that are built once and forgotten. They're slow on mobile, invisible to Google, designed to simply show existence. Over time, they become a silent liability, casting doubt on an otherwise honest business.

The standard web design options also fall short. On one end are low-cost, template-driven WordPress builds that fail at the foundational mechanics. On the other are high-dollar agencies encumbered by process, overhead, and jargon. Both approaches miss the point. Your website isn't a vanity project; in fact, it isn't really about you at all. It's for your visitors. It's digital real estate tasked with earning their trust.

An effective website functions as a commercial instrument. It helps the right audience find you, understand your offer quickly, and act. For most businesses, this requires just 3 to 5 fast, well-structured pages.

Then there's the DIY path. While anyone can publish a site, building something that actually performs requires precise execution — to attract qualified visitors, load instantly, and rank on Google. Done right, your website becomes a durable asset.

// The System

Website3000 is my alternative to the standard mess. Every site is hand-coded — meaning I don't use templates, themes or heavy frameworks. I write every line of code in semantic HTML, CSS and JavaScript. This requires technical depth, can be tedious, and is therefore an uncommon approach. Yet it yields a simpler, deterministic system that's bulletproof: no bloated layers, no databases to hack, and no plugins to crash your site next month. Just a lean, clean website engineered to load fast, capture enquiries, and render perfectly on any device.

This engineered simplicity extends to how the site is maintained. The subscription model exists because a website should remain a worry-free utility as your business grows. Fast content updates, secure hosting, and performance monitoring are included — giving you an on-call developer to make sure things run smoothly.

// The Standard

What you get is a custom website that loads in under a second, scores 95–100 on Google PageSpeed, and is designed to help your business. You also work directly with the person who wrote the code — no account managers, no call waiting, no support tickets.

This is independent practice by design. Without the layers of typical agency management, I ensure every client receives uncompromised attention.
